Demon Slayer Characters

Tanjiro Kamado is the kind and determined protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, who becomes a Demon Slayer after a demon attack kills his family and turns his sister Nezuko into a demon. He possesses a strong sense of compassion, a heightened sense of smell for tracking demons, and uses a unique combat style combining the Water Breathing technique with his family's Hinokami Kagura dance. His singular goal is to find a way to turn Nezuko back into a human

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a central character in the anime and manga series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. Known for his cowardly and anxious personality, he is one of the traveling companions of the protagonist, Tanjiro Kamado. Despite his initial anxieties, his love for others and determination to honor his mentor motivate him to overcome fear and defeat demons, ultimately mastering advanced forms of his lightning-based sword style and becoming a loyal and capable warrior

Nezuko Kamado is the younger sister of Tanjiro Kamado and a central character in Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. After her family is slaughtered by a demon, she is transformed into one, but remarkably retains her humanity and consciousness. She joins her brother on his questto become a demon slayer and find a cure for her condition. Nezuko is unique among demons for her ability to control her demonic urges, protect humans, fight with enhanced strength and pyrokinesis, and eventually develop immunity to sunlight.
