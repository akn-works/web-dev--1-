Demon Slayer Characters

Tanjiro Kamado is the kind and determined protagonist of Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba, who becomes a Demon Slayer after a demon attack kills his family and turns his sister Nezuko into a demon. He possesses a strong sense of compassion, a heightened sense of smell for tracking demons, and uses a unique combat style combining the Water Breathing technique with his family's Hinokami Kagura dance. His singular goal is to find a way to turn Nezuko back into a human

Zenitsu Agatsuma is a central character in the anime and manga series Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba. Known for his cowardly and anxious personality, he is one of the traveling companions of the protagonist, Tanjiro Kamado. Despite his fearful demeanor, he is a powerful Demon Slayer.
